by Darshil Dave

How many people agree that Movie "Life of Pi" deserves more Oscars?

I'm afraid I'm not one of them. Though an excellent and very moving film, the only awards I would agree that Life of Pi deserved to win was Best Director and Best Visual Effects. I felt that Best Original Score should have gone to either Argo or Skyfall, primarily because both those films have moments of tension and action, but also moments of thoughtfulness and reflection. The music is composed well enough to cover both emotional moments. Life of Pi however, while a great score, is pretty much centred around moments of emptiness and calm. As for it's win for Best Cinematography, not even remotely deserved considering that most of the lighting in that film has been created digitally. However in the film's defence, I do think it should have won Best Adapted Screenplay. Though Argo was the better film, I felt Life of Pi was an incredibly impressive adaptation of a very difficult book.
